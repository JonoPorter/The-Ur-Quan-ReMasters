Controls:

Player 1:
Fire: Enter
Special: Ctrl
Move Forward: Up Arrow
Turn Left: Left Arrow
Turn Right: Right Arrow

Player 2:
Fire: A
Special: W
Move Forward: E
Turn Left: S
Turn Right: F

Others:

Quick-Save: F5
Quick-Load: F6

To Select a new ship once you have died just double click on the desired ship.
